SolOpen hand, palm down, fingers spread · Strong, open
LaHand on shoulder, fingers pointing out · Relaxed
TiPointing finger upward · Rising tension
Do'Fist pointing up · High, resolved

The story

Solfai was built by a high school choir student who struggled with sight reading and couldn't find a tool that actually helped.

Most apps just play notes at you. Solfai explains the music to you — like having a choir director in your pocket, available any time you need to prepare.
